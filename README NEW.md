## 📌 Project Overview

This is a personal **Data Analysis project** that involves a comprehensive analysis of **US Airline Flight Delays and Cancellations in 2015**. Using **Power BI**, an interactive dashboard was built to uncover patterns in flight delays, cancellations, and on-time performance across different airlines, cities, and months.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall flight performance across US airlines in 2015
- Identify key reasons for flight cancellations
- Track monthly trends in delays and cancellations
- Compare delay rates across different airlines
- Provide actionable insights into flight schedule performance

## 💡 Key Insights

- 📌 Only **57.99%** of flights were on-time in 2015
- 📌 **Weather** was the biggest reason for cancellations at **57.3%**
- 📌 **United Air Lines** had the highest delay rate at **53.46%**
- 📌 **Hawaiian Airlines** had the lowest delay rate at **24.17%**
- 📌 **Delta Air Lines** operated the most flights at **352K**
- 📌 Delays peaked in **July 2015** at **47.61%**
- 📌 On-time performance was best in **September 2015** at **69.55%**
